# Smart Research Assistant

An AI agent skill for searching structured sources (OKF bundles, local folders, Notion) for relevant information.

## The Process

1. Clarify the research topic
2. Identify the most relevant sources (OKF bundles, local files, web)
3. Extract core information
4. Create a structured summary with source citations
5. Save results for later retrieval

## Use Cases

- Developers researching new technologies
- Writers gathering background information
- Analysts compiling market research
- Students writing papers with proper citations

## Best Practices

- Always verify sources before citing
- Prioritize OKF bundles for structured knowledge
- Flag conflicting information for human review
