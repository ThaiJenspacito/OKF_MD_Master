# OKF Social & Growth Strategy

A comprehensive social media and community growth plan for OKF MD Master.

## Platforms

- **GitHub**: Primary hub. Stars, forks, issues, milestones, discussions
- **LinkedIn**: Professional network. Company page, launch posts
- **Facebook**: Business page. Community updates, events
- **WhatsApp**: Direct chat. Quick support, feedback
- **Telegram**: Bot @JensBeckerBot. Commands, notifications
- **Discord**: Community server. Channels: general, skills, help, dev

## Growth Phases

1. **First 25 Stars** (Now): Personal network, direct outreach
2. **First 100 Stars** (1 month): LinkedIn/X posts, Reddit, HackerNews, DEV.to
3. **First 25 Contributors** (2 months): Good first issues, community skills

## Content Strategy

- Post 3x/week on LinkedIn: OKF tips, agent demos, progress updates
- 1x/week tutorial blog post
- Weekly changelog on GitHub
- Community spotlight: feature top contributors

## Metrics

- GitHub Stars: target 100 in 30 days
- Discord members: target 25 in 30 days
- External PRs: target 2 in 60 days
